Project Overview: Collaborated on a research paper analyzing factors affecting movie ratings using various statistical methods. The goal was to identify correlations and assess the significance of variables such as genre, director, budget, and release date on the rating of the movie.

Statistical Methods and Analysis: Employed techniques including ANOVA, Cook's Distance, bootstrapping, and tests for normality and variance using R and RStudio to ensure accurate and valid findings. Created visualizations like box plots and scatter plots to illustrate data relationships.

Collaborative Writing and Tools: Utilized Overleaf for seamless collaborative writing and editing within a LaTeX environment. Designed supplementary materials using HTML, CSS, and Bootstrap for interactive and visually appealing presentations

Model Validation and Reporting: Conducted rigorous model validation tests to ensure reliability. Applied CSS formatting in Overleaf to produce a polished and professional research paper adhering to academic standards.

Skill Development: Enhanced proficiency in statistical analysis, data visualization, and collaborative writing. Gained experience in R programming, advanced statistical techniques, and effective presentation of research findings, improving teamwork and communication skills through the project
